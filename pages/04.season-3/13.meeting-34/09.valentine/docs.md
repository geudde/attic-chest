<div class="author">Miss Richardson</div>

##### To Ellen

If thou, my Ellen, wilt receive  
A Heart long anxious to be thine;  
Love’s beauteous Garland will I weave  
And crown thee, Sweet, my Valentine.

Florio

Feb<sup>y</sup> 11th
